Vision

Collective for Solidarity Health e.V.

A health-promoting place of encounter, exchange, and networking for all people in Lichtenberg. With café, spaces for workshops, groups, and consultations to strengthen community well-being and social participation.

Who We Are

Who We Are

We are a group of people with the common wish to make health care in the neighborhood more holistic and social. For this purpose, we have been working as Collective for Solidarity Health e.V. on the implementation of a neighborhood health center in Berlin. We are guided by existing structures such as GeKo Neukölln and Poliklinik Veddel in Hamburg, with whom we exchange ideas through the nationwide Poliklinik Syndikat.

The Concept

The Concept

Body and soul, where and how we live, and our place in society collectively influence our health and quality of life. Viewing and strengthening these as a whole is a crucial aspect of our neighborhood health center idea. Specifically, this means close collaboration between various professional groups within healthcare (such as doctors from different specialties, psychologists, therapists, nursing professionals) as well as social workers and people active in legal, educational, and activist fields. It is particularly important to us that neighborhood residents can actively participate in the development and design of the center, creating a place that is adapted to their needs and grows with them.

Our Goal

Our Goal

Our goal is to promote health - that is, the physical, mental, and social well-being - of neighborhood residents and offer a place of encounter, exchange, and networking (e.g., in the form of a café, spaces for workshops and groups). Currently, our work focuses on building initial care offerings in Lichtenberg, but we aim to bring the neighborhood health center concept to all Berlin neighborhoods where residents can be particularly empowered.

Take Part

Take Part

We are a relatively homogeneous, white, academically socialized group and want more perspectives to meet the requirements of the people in the neighborhood! We look forward to everyone who gets to know us, get an idea of us and want to enrich us with your individual perspective and your experiences! If you would like to become active with us or want to support us, have a warm invitation to stop by at the next meeting or to contact us via our email address :)
